Design For America

Design For America is a student group that aims to tackle real world problems through human-centered design thinking. The WashU Studio is divided into teams of five or six students, and each semester, a topic is provided. Through user research, each group identifies an issue within that topic and come up with a creative solution to that problem. Topics are often relevant to better understanding the community at large and each group must work with a community group throughout the semester.

The goal of each semester is to be ready to prototype the solution and possibly move to implementation during the next semester. I have been a part of Design For America since August 2014 and am currently working on two teams that have reached implementation. Visit DFA National.
