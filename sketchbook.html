Working On Site

Using the backdrop of Florence, Italy, we learned to sketch quickly on location, implying complexity but not drawing every single detail. For the final project, we selected either a place, event, or action, and illustrated a book based on that activity in Florence.

❮ ❯

Bars In Florence | I focused on bar culture in Italy and how it differs from American coffee culture. I drew my favorite spots in the city, adding commentary about why they're so great and my reommendations. My book has two versions: a fully-colored version and a coloring book version!
