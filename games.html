Games People Play

Mock-ups of an imaginary game app, created with Adobe Illustrator.

We were given four different prompts from which to choose, and from that we designed characters, environments, and simple menus for how our game would look. The prompt I chose was Berry Patch, which included a forest and lake environment, a bunny and kniving fox character, and candy and fruit that the player collects.
