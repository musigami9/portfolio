Visual Journalism & Reportage

An ongoing series of pages scanned from my sketchbooks for the class Visual Journalism & Reportage, taught by Douglas Dowd.

The goals of this course are to embrace journalistic practices and values, including observational accuracy, sourced reporting, strict standards of nonfiction and consideration of the public interest. During the first half of the course, we will research and work toward sustained sketchbook work in the field, exploring people, places, and methods. We will compile a zine of our work and process. In the second half of the course, we will be reporting on a topic of our choosing and producing a writing piece paired with a visual element.
