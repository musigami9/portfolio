We Open in Three Weeks!

We will imagine that we have been tasked with redesigning the identity of a restaurant in Florence and that our client would like to reopen three weeks from today. What makes this place different from other restaurants, especially those restaurants that have a similar menu, location, theme, etc?

Logotype | I wanted to emphasize the restaurant's history as one of the older and more established bakeries around Florence (Vecchio Forno literally means "Old Bakery") and give the type a hand-written treatment. Their current logo features a combination of the Duomo, a fleur-de-lis, and a wheat stalk. In retaining some sense of their current identity, I chose to involve the wheat stalk into the new logotype.

Menu mock-up | The idea behind the online menu was meant to support Vecchio Forno's rotating menu that changes daily. Rather than a physical paper menu that would be costly and inefficient to change daily, the online menu could easily be switched from day to day. View the working site here.

Business card | Logotype, location, and hours on one side with a bolder version of the website illustration on the back side.
